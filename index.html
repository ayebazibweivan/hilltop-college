Courses We Offer

Each category of courses offers a diverse range of subjects and skills to cater to your individual interests and career goals. Whether you're looking to build a strong foundation in a particular field, gain specialized knowledge, or embark on a journey of personal and professional growth, Texas College's courses are designed to empower you for success.

Bachelor's Degrees

These are typically three to four-year programs that provide a foundational education in a specific field. Common degrees include Bachelor of Arts (B.A.), Bachelor of Science (B.S.), and Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA).

Master's Degrees:

Postgraduate education offers more advanced study and specialization. Master's programs, such as Master of Arts (M.A.) and Master of Science (M.S.), typically last one to two years and focus on in-depth knowledge, research, and practical skills in a specific area.

Major-Specific Courses

As students progress in their undergraduate studies, they delve deeper into their chosen field of study, taking major-specific courses that build expertise and specialization.

Our facilities

HILLTOP boasts of it's facilities which are designed to enhance the overall student experience, fostering a well-rounded environment that promotes academic success, physical well-being, and a sense of community. The world-class library encourages intellectual growth, the largest playground provides ample space for recreation, and the cafeteria ensures that students have access to both delightful and nutritious meals, creating a campus that supports every aspect of student life.

World Class Library

HILLTOP COLLEGE boasts a world-class library, a haven for students and researchers. With an extensive collection of books, digital resources, and research materials, the library serves as the academic heartbeat of the campus. It's a quiet sanctuary for deep study, collaborative projects, and exploration of a vast array of knowledge.

Largest Playground

Our campus takes pride in offering the largest playground, providing students with a spacious and vibrant outdoor area for recreation and sports. From impromptu soccer matches to leisurely strolls, the playground is a dynamic space for students to unwind, socialize, and embrace an active lifestyle.

Tasty and Healthy Food

The campus cafeteria is not just a dining area; it's a culinary experience. Our chefs curate a menu that blends deliciousness with nutritional value, offering a variety of tasty and healthy options. From nourishing salads to flavorful entrees, students can enjoy meals that cater to diverse tastes and dietary preferences.
